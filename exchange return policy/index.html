FAQ
Can I return or exchange a Decode Age product that is past its expiration date?

In general, Decode Age does not refund or exchange products that are past their expiration date. However, if you have a professional wholesale account with us, please contact Decode Age's Customer Service Department for further information.

I bought one of your products but I am not satisfied with it, but my doctor won't take it back because it has been opened.

Please call our Customer Service Department at 9009003095, and we will do our best to accommodate your situation.

I am a health-care practitioner and one of my patients returned one of your products. Can I receive credit for this product?

Yes. Decode Age will accept a product return or exchange from one of your patients. The best way to do this is to contact Decode Age while the patient is in your office so we can obtain the information we need about why the product was returned.

Can I return a product that I purchased and shipped internationally?

Decode Age currently does not accept returns on internationally shipped products. All international product sales are final.

How do I return or exchange a product?

To return or exchange a product that you purchased from Decode Age or from a health-care practitioner, please call our Customer Service Department at 9009003095 or email care@decodeage.com to receive specific instructions on returns and exchanges.

What if I received a damaged or incorrect product?

If you received a damaged or incorrect product, please contact our Customer Service Department at 9009003095 or email care@decodeage.com within 48 hours of receiving the product. Provide photos of the damaged/incorrect product, and we will work with you to resolve the issue as quickly as possible.

How long does it take to process a refund?

For online payments, we will refund the amount back to the original source (debit/credit card, bank, wallet, etc) within 7 working days of receiving the request from you. For COD orders, we will refund the amount to your bank account within 14 working days of requesting a refund.

Can I cancel or modify my order?

If you wish to cancel or modify your order, please contact our Customer Service Department at 9009003095 or email care@decodeage.com as soon as possible. We will do our best to accommodate your request; however, once an order has been processed for shipping, we cannot guarantee any changes or cancellations.

What if I have a reaction to one of your products?

If you experience a reaction to one of our products, discontinue use immediately and consult your healthcare practitioner. You can also contact our Customer Service Department at 9009003095 or email care@decodeage.com to report the reaction and receive further guidance.

Do you offer any product samples?

At the moment, we do not offer product samples. We recommend consulting with your healthcare practitioner or our Customer Service Department for guidance on selecting the most suitable product for your needs.

Where can I find more information about your products and their ingredients?

Detailed information about our products and their ingredients can be found on our website. You can also contact our Customer Service Department at 9009003095 or email care@decodeage.com for further assistance.
